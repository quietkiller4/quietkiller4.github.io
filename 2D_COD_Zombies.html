This is the title screen of the game you can click on the start game then you it will take you into the game. You can click on the story and see the story of the game. You can click on options and it will take you to that page. You also can click on quit game to stop the game.

This is the options page and you can go back to the title screen by hitting the x. You can also increase and decrease the sound by hitting the + or the -.

You try to survive by killing the zombies and completing the story. You can go around and buy weapons and perks to help you survive, but the only way you get out is by completing the story.
