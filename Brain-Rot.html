You had to come up with an idea and make it into a reality. We came up with an idea to have a playlist of movies from Disney-Plus, Netflix, and Hulu. Once you build your playlist, the shows will move in order from show to show even if it is on a different platform. This code will automatically sign you into each account and play the list you build seamlessly.

What I did was setup the Hulu account to sign in and play the show that was on the playlist. I also combined all the code from Netflix, Hulu, and Disney-Plus into one file so they all work.
